Synopsis
A relentless bully continuously torments and crushes the hearts and hopes of their victims. The constant abuse breeds a deep sense of resentment and a desire for revenge in those who suffer. As the pressure mounts, one of the victims, pushed to their breaking point, decides to fight back. This act of defiance leads to an unforeseen and tragic event, altering the lives of everyone involved forever.
